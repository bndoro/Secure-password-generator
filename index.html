Student Safe Password Generator

Create memorable, policy-compliant passwords and passphrases for everyday use.

Important password safety reminders

  • Do not use personal information (names, birthdays, student ID, etc.).
  • Use passphrases (multiple words) you can remember, e.g., BirdsSwimCoffee_Purple or Battery21BladePixelCup.
  • Do not reuse passwords across websites.
  • Use a password manager; protect it with a strong master password.
  • Avoid easy words like password. Use uncommon words instead.
  • Do not share passwords; use a shared password manager vault for shared accounts.
  • Review accounts yearly and delete old/unused accounts.

Password requirements (enforced)

  • Minimum 12 characters; maximum 32 characters
  • Must include at least 3 of 4 groups: uppercase, lowercase, digits, special characters
  • Spaces allowed, but none at the beginning or end
  • Blocks not-allowed words (example: password)
