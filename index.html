Student Safe Password Generator

Memorable, policy-compliant passwords you can say, type, and remember.

Important reminders

  • Avoid personal info (names, birthdays, student ID, school name).
  • Prefer passphrases / story-mode phrases you can remember and say out loud.
  • Do not reuse passwords across sites.
  • Use a password manager when possible.

Optional: personal info warning

If you enter personal info, we warn if it appears in the result.

Requirements (enforced)

  • Minimum 12 characters, maximum 32
  • At least 3 of 4 groups: uppercase, lowercase, digits, special characters
  • Spaces allowed, but not at the beginning or end
  • Blocks common/banned words (optional)

Example: BirdsSwimCoffee_Purple21!

Leave the word list blank to use built-in uncommon words.

Example: TheGrumpyToasterBitMyCloud21!
